Nutrition N Beyond

To have more control on your blood sugar, make sure you are eating leafy greens everyday. Our favorite pick is what we call "The Dal Palak Bowl". Take a salad bowl, and fill the bottom 1/3rd portion with a Dal (you can change the type of dal every day). Fill the next layer with sauteed spinach (slightly salted, sauteed in little oil) - the spinach should cover 1/3rd of the bowl. At the top, you can add your choice of grilled protein - eggs, chicken, paneer, or fish.

Get Groovin

One of the best activities you can do to stay healthy is to do a brisk walk. How do you know if your walking is brisk enough? If you are able to talk with some breathlessness, then you are walking at a brisk pace. Don't overstrain yourself though. And remember, 30 minutes. Every single day.

Mind Magic

When you feel stressed at work or after, have a glass of lemon juice (preferably without added sugar) or an orange as a snack. Vitamin C helps you relieve stress and also helps boost your immunity. You can also try a chamomile tea, if you are a tea-drinker! Remember, stress elevates your blood glucose level.
